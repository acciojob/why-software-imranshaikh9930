Why I want to become a developer?

As a developer, you get to create software and applications that can solve real-world problems and enhance people's lives. The process of designing elegant solutions to complex problems can be intellectually stimulating and satisfying. echnology is constantly evolving, and developers play a key role in driving innovation. If you're passionate about being at the forefront of technological advancements and contributing to the latest trends, becoming a developer could be the right path for you.
